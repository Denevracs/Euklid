Use the latest `main` branch for security updates. Tagged releases will inherit fixes as they are published.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

1. Email **security@euclid.network** with the subject line `SECURITY: <summary>`.
2. Provide:
   - A detailed description of the issue
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Potential impact assessment
   - Suggested remediation (if known)
3. The team will acknowledge receipt within **3 business days** and provide a timeline for remediation after triage.
4. Please do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until a fix is released.

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will credit reporters in release notes unless otherwise requested.
